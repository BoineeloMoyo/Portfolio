Introduction & Objectives

A Conceptual Analysis of Green Spaces in Kirchheim unter Teck This project explores the distribution, accessibility, and significance of green free spaces in the suburban municipality of Kirchheim unter Teck. It examines how these spaces contribute to the happiness and well-being of residents, focusing on the interplay between private, shared, and public free spaces. The study combines qualitative and quantitative methods to assess the spatial and atmospheric qualities of these spaces, aiming to provide insights into their role as places of mental and physical contentment. By analyzing urban patterns across selected neighborhoods, the project offers a deeper understanding of suburban green infrastructure and its implications for quality of life.
